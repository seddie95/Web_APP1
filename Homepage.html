Welcome

The Rugby World Cup Hosted once every four years, is rugby's centrepiece event. It is the big stage that rugby players from all over the world dream of reaching. Held over the course of seven weeks, it is the 3rd largest sports event in the world after the summer olympics and the Football World Cup.

  • Dates & Format

    The Rugby World Cup begins on the 20th September and runs until the 2nd November 2019.

    Pool stages: 4 pools of 5 teams, 40 pool matches in total.
    Finals: Quarter finals, semi finals, bronze final, finals

    The full schedule of fixtutures can be found here.

  • Teams

    There are 20 teams participating in the rugby world cup. This includes teams from Europe, Asia, North America, South America, Africa and Australasia.

    To see the Current rank of each the competing teams click here.

  • Stadia

    All 48 of the Rugby World Cup matches will be played in 12 venues across the whole of Japan.

    The final will be played on the 2nd November 2019 in Yokohama Stadium, Kanagawa.
